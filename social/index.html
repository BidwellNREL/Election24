 Quick Information

Join us for a night of bowling, conversation, and food at Lucky Strike bowling. This is a free event with the option to provide your contact information to the sponsors in exchange for hardware credits on AWS and possibly some swag. For the social event we will be in the VIP section with 4 lanes and will have non-alcoholic drinks and a Fajita style spread for food. Attendees are welcome to purchase alcoholic drinks from the bar.

Ansys and AWS will be presenting on a cloud platform tool to speed up the time it takes to go from idea to computation that integrates and deploys easily with AWS compute resources. This presentation will last 10 minutes after which the bowling and conversation will commence.
